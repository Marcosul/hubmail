## 2) Standard Email Account Conventions

For each domain, create initially the following accounts:

### 📧 Operations / System

- `admin@<domain>`: general administration (root)
- `no-reply@<domain>`: automated emails (no-reply)
- `noreply@<domain>`: fallback (hyphenless version)
- `system@<domain>`: internal events / logs / integrations
- `alerts@<domain>`: critical alerts (infra, security)

### 📧 Support / Customer

- `contact@<domain>`: general contact
- `support@<domain>`: technical support
- `help@<domain>`: friendly support alternative
- `success@<domain>`: customer success / onboarding
- `onboarding@<domain>`: customer onboarding
- `feedback@<domain>`: suggestions and improvements

### 📧 Sales / Revenue

- `sales@<domain>`: sales
- `growth@<domain>`: growth / acquisition
- `partners@<domain>`: international partnerships
- `affiliate@<domain>`: affiliate program

### 📧 Finance

- `finance@<domain>`: general finance
- `billing@<domain>`: recurring billing
- `invoices@<domain>`: invoicing
- `payments@<domain>`: payments
- `tax@<domain>`: tax / fiscal matters

### 📧 Marketing / Communication

- `marketing@<domain>`: general marketing
- `media@<domain>`: press / media relations
- `social@<domain>`: social media
- `newsletter@<domain>`: content distribution
- `events@<domain>`: events management

### 📧 Product / Technology

- `dev@<domain>`: development
- `devops@<domain>`: infrastructure operations
- `tech@<domain>`: general technology
- `security@<domain>`: security / vulnerabilities
- `abuse@<domain>`: abuse reports / compliance
- `infra@<domain>`: infrastructure
- `api@<domain>`: integrations / API support

### 📧 Administrative / Internal

- `hr@<domain>`: human resources
- `careers@<domain>`: job openings
- `jobs@<domain>`: recruitment
- `legal@<domain>`: legal affairs
- `compliance@<domain>`: regulatory compliance
- `exec@<domain>`: executive / board

## 3) Initial Password Policy

- **Initial Password:** generate a strong random password (min. 20 chars) per mailbox.
- **Mandatory Change:** required after the first login for personal accounts.
